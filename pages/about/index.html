About

I am Robin Schroer, also known as sulami. This is my blog, where I discuss various topics, mostly related to software development, but occasionally also others. I am a nix guy by heart and mainly write Clojure, Python, and Haskell. I have been contributing to the Linux kernel as well as writing an X11 window manager and I generally like experimenting with code.

I also love puzzles/contests, so here are some I have completed:

  • Project Euler 1–100 (And a bunch more)
  • The Eudyptula Kernel Challenge
  • The Matasano Crypto Challenges
  • Hacktoberfest 2015–2018
  • Advent of Code 2015-2018
  • The Monad Challenges
  • The Deadlock Empire

Apart from that, I am in the 94th percentile of Functional Languages on HackerRank, using Haskell, and in the 95th honor percentile on CodeWars, using mostly Haskell and Python.
